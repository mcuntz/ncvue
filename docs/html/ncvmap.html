ncvue.ncvmap#

Map panel of ncvue.

The panel allows plotting contour or mesh maps of georeferenced data. Maps can be animated along the time axis.

This module was written by Matthias Cuntz while at Institut National de Recherche pour lAgriculture, lAlimentation et lEnvironnement (INRAE), Nancy, France.
